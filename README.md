## Hello, I'm Moka! 👋🏼


As a dedicated Full-Stack Engineer with over 9 years of professional experience, I am passionate about building robust, high-impact web applications that solve complex problems.

My career has evolved from a deep specialization in frontend (using React and Vue.js) to my current role as a Founding Full-Stack Engineer in the health-tech industry. I am now building a sophisticated Radiology Information System (PACS) from the ground up, using Node.js, React, and TypeScript. This has been a fascinating opportunity to dive deep into a new domain and learn specialized protocols like DICOM.

My core strength lies in my adaptability and my drive to tackle new challenges, whether in logistics, Web3/Blockchain, or now, healthcare.

I am currently based in Bandung, Indonesia (🇮🇩) and am open to remote collaborations across the globe, especially those pushing technology forward.
